What platform should this run on?

This affects everything — tech stack, how you access it, offline support, and deployment.

A

Web App (Browser)

Access from any device via a URL. Easy to update, works on your laptop and phone. Requires internet or a local server to run.

B

Desktop App (Windows)

Runs locally on your PC. Works fully offline, faster, can access local files easily. Only available on your Windows machine.

C

Mobile App

On your phone at all times — good for flashcards and quick review. Harder to build, harder to type long notes on.
